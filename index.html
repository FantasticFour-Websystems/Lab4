Gamma Nu Eta (ΓΝΗ or GNH) is the National Honor Society for Information Techonology. It was organized to recognize and promote excellence in the study and the leadership of Information Technology, to offer knowledge and services to its members’ schools and surrounding communities; and to play an active role in the application and evolution of Information Technology within society. The organization provides academic services and runs career related events as a part of its mission to achieve these goals
